Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get started?

You can set up your practice profile and start using AYUSHCare within 24 hours of signing up. Our onboarding team will guide you through the process.

Is my patient data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption, are HIPAA compliant, and conduct regular third-party security audits to ensure your data is protected.

Can I use AYUSHCare on mobile devices?

Absolutely. AYUSHCare is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 customer support via email, phone, and live chat. Plus dedicated onboarding assistance and regular training webinars.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required.

Can I integrate with my existing systems?

Yes. AYUSHCare offers integrations with popular calendar apps, payment gateways, lab systems, and provides RESTful APIs for custom integrations.
